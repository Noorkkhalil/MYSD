About Us

Muslim Youth of San Diego is a non-profit organization solely ran by the youth for the youth. Together, we plan events to unite our young adults and tie their hearts to the masjid. Shed the light on the future generations. Light the way.

What We Do

  • Bonfires
  • Scholarly Lectures
  • Festivals
  • Qiyam Al-Layl
  • Travel
  • Sports Activities
  • Charity Work
  • Religious Retreats
  • Movie Nights

MYSD BROTHERS

BEACH HIKE 4/14/2022

Elyas

Joined: March 2021

After MYSD I found myself having a reason to go to the masjid for more than just jummuah and taraweeh. I found myself spending more time there weather it was for the talks at the bonfires or just hanging out with the brothers and helping out at other events.

Mara

Joined: Feb 2022

I first moved to San Diego as a new grad, knowing next to no one except my in-laws. I spent a lot of time calling friends from home or talking to people online since it was still pandemic mode and in-person events weren’t common yet. Until one day, I went to a MYSD Sisters’ Brunch. I had seen a few faces from Jummah, but overall didn’t truly know any of the sisters. After just an hour or so of talking, the ice was broken and I felt comfortable reaching out. I started reaching out to sisters one on one and exchanging hugs at Jummah. If it wasn’t for MYSD, I wouldn’t have gotten to know the caring girls I call my friends. Even if you arrive with no one, you’ll leave with a new friend.

Baraa

Joined: June 2021

Joining MYSD has been a life changing experience for me. Through their weekly events, I was able to build bonds with amazing people while also strengthening my own faith and coming closer to Allah swt.

Samina

Joined: December 2021

10 months ago I moved to San Diego and didn’t know anyone here. I am grateful to MYSD for connecting me to the Muslim community and helping me build a great circle of friends. MYSD has made my new home actually feel like home!

Husnaa

By attending events hosted through MYSD, I have not only become a more knowledgeable Muslim, but have made friends that have shown me unconditional love and support. I greatly value the tools that MYSD has equipped me with that allow me to contribute to the Muslim community while bettering myself as an individual.
